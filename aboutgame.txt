This is a classic brick breaker game

Controls:

LEFT_ARROW: Move the paddle to the left
RIGHT_ARROW: Move the paddle to the right
Initially adjust paddle position and hit
  space bar to start.
Power-ups appear on screen at different times.
Catching power-up enables paddle to shoot bullets.
UP_ARROW: To shoot bullets.

Scoring: 

Hitting each brick other than Green Brick gives 25.
Yellow Brick vanishes on being hit.
Blue Brick becomes Yellow on being hit.
Ball cannot harm Green Brick.
But, Bullet can pass through Green Brick.
